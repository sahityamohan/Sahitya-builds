About Our School

St. Mary's Convent School is a premier educational institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence and character development. With a team of experienced educators and modern facilities, we provide a nurturing environment where students can thrive both academically and personally.


Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that will help them become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow. We believe in holistic development, balancing academics with sports, arts, and extracurricular activities.
